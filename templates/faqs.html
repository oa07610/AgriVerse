Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about AgriVerse

Account Management

How do I create a new account?

Click on the "Register" link on the login page, fill in the required details including your username, email, and password, and submit the form. You'll receive a verification code via email to complete your registration.

I didn't receive a verification email. What should I do?

Please check your spam or junk folder. If you still can't find the email, ensure that you entered the correct email address during signup. If the issue persists, contact our support team for assistance.

Using the Dashboard

What crops and regions are available for price predictions?

Currently, our dashboard supports Sugar and Maize crops across the Punjab, Sindh, and KPK regions. Future updates may include more crops and regions.

How often is the price data updated?

Our historical data is updated annually, and predicted prices are refreshed quarterly based on market trends and analysis.

Features and Functionalities

What are "Market Insights"?

Market Insights provide the latest updates on crop prices and market trends, helping you make informed decisions based on current data.

Can I export the data from the Dashboard?

Currently, data export functionality is under development. Stay tuned for upcoming updates that will allow you to download the data in various formats.

Technical Support

Which browsers are supported by AgriVerse?

AgriVerse is optimized for the latest versions of modern browsers such as Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. For the best experience, ensure your browser is up to date.
